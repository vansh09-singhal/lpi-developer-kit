# LifeAtlas Contributor Program — Participant Agreement

**Between:** WINNIIO AB (org.nr 559350-0722), Gothenburg, Sweden ("WINNIIO")
**And:** ______________________________ ("Participant")
**Program dates:** May 4 — June 27, 2026 (8 weeks)

## 1. Program Description

The Participant joins the LifeAtlas Contributor Program, a remote internship program where participants contribute to the LifeAtlas digital twin platform through one of five tracks: Agent Builders, Content & Research, Design & UX, 3D & Visualization, or QA & Security.

## 2. Intellectual Property

### 2.1 Contributions to WINNIIO Repositories
All code, content, designs, research, documentation, and other work product submitted as Pull Requests to WINNIIO-owned GitHub repositories (under the Life-Atlas organization) is owned by WINNIIO AB upon merge. Such contributions are licensed under the MIT License as part of the open-source project.

### 2.2 Personal Repositories
Code and work maintained in the Participant's personal GitHub repositories remains the Participant's property. By submitting a link to such work as part of the program, the Participant grants WINNIIO AB a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, modify, and distribute that work under the MIT License.

### 2.3 Content, Research, and Design Work
Written content (case studies, course modules, reports), research outputs, visual designs, and presentation materials produced as part of program tasks are work-for-hire and owned by WINNIIO AB. The Participant retains the right to reference this work in their portfolio and for academic credit.

### 2.4 Attribution
The Participant will be credited as a contributor in relevant project files, commit histories, and where applicable, in published materials.

## 3. Confidentiality

The Participant agrees not to share, publish, or disclose:
- WINNIIO internal source code (beyond the public open-source repositories)
- Customer or partner information encountered during the program
- Internal business strategy, financial information, or unreleased product plans
- Access credentials, API keys, or infrastructure details

This obligation survives the end of the program.

## 4. Expected Conduct

- Attend daily standups as scheduled (camera on)
- Deliver work product at least weekly
- Communicate blockers promptly
- Treat fellow participants and team members with respect
- Follow the code of conduct in the program repository

## 5. Termination

Either party may end participation at any time with written notice. WINNIIO may remove a Participant who fails to meet conduct or delivery expectations after one written warning. All IP provisions (Section 2) and confidentiality obligations (Section 3) survive termination.

## 6. Compensation

This is an unpaid internship program. The Participant receives:
- Internship completion certificate (upon completing the program)
- Letter of recommendation (based on contributions)
- Published open-source work on their GitHub profile
- No monetary compensation is provided

## 7. Academic Credit

WINNIIO will provide documentation required by the Participant's educational institution for academic credit, including: weekly progress records, contribution reports, and a signed completion certificate.
